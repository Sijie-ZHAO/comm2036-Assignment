Gong Li

Chinese-Singaporean actress

1.Overview

Gong Li is a Chinese Singaporean actress.Regarded as one of the best actresses in China today,
she is known for her outstanding appearence and naturalistic performance.She starred in three of the four
Chinese-language films that have been nominated for the Academy Award for Best International Feature Film.

  • Born:December 31,1965(age 58 years),Shenyang,China
  • Nationality:Chinese,Singaporean
  • Height:1.69m
  • Spouse:Jean-Michel Jarre(m.2019),Ooi Hoe Soeng(m.1996-2009)
  • Representative Works:《Red Sorghum》《Farewell to My Concubine》《Alive》
  • Main Achievements:

    Best Actress Award at the 49th Venice International Film Festival
    Two Golden Rooster Awards for Best Actress
    Two popular film Hundred Flowers Award for Best Actress
    The 26th Hong Kong Film Awards Best Actress Award
    Best Actress Award at the 24th Montreal International Film Festival
    Best Actress Award, 1st Varna International Film Festival
    Golden Camera Award at the 43rd Berlin International Film Festival
    Special Grand Prize, 57th Cannes International Film Festival
